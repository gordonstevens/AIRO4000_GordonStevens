> ## System on a Chip (SoC) Manufacturers

- [Raspberry Pi](https://www.raspberrypi.com/): The OG SoC manufacturer
- [ODROID by Hardkernel](https://www.hardkernel.com/): High quality, been around for ages, overspecs whatever RPi puts out
- [Banana Pi](https://banana-pi.org/): They have some massive boards with a good [list of places to buy](https://banana-pi.org/en/buy/)
- [Intel NUC](https://grokipedia.com/page/Next_Unit_of_Computing):
Discontinued, were baller for their time. Used Intel chipsets instead of the usual rockchip/mediatek stuff

> ## Electronics parts suppliers (Canada)

- [DigiKey Canada](https://www.digikey.ca/): Very big supplier, headquartered in USA, has a Canadian website.
- [Mouser Electronics](https://www.mouser.ca/): Based in Kitchener, Ontario
- [Sayal Electronics & Hobbies](https://secure.sayal.com/): Toronto. They have a lot of tools, make custom kits, etc.
- [A-1 Electronic Parts](https://www.a1parts.ca/): Toronto. Crappy website, been in business forever. Sell a ton of stuff.
